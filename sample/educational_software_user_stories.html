Educational Software Use Cases & User Stories

Student Lifecycle System

Admissions & Enrollment
Prospective students apply via a branded admissions portal. Documents are uploaded, interviews scheduled using an appointment booking system, and acceptance letters are sent via automated emails.
Onboarding & Orientation
New students receive login details to mobile/web portals, access welcome videos, register for courses, and attend virtual open days scheduled via the event manager.
Support & Retention
Students book support appointments through a ticketing or calendar system. AI-powered chatbots assist with FAQs, while alerts notify staff about students showing signs of disengagement.
Graduation & Alumni Engagement
Graduation events are managed via the events module. Alumni are invited to join exclusive networks, and newsletters are automatically sent via CRM integrations.

Learning Management System (LMS)

Course Delivery
Instructors upload lecture materials, create quizzes and assignments, and track student progress. Students access content via a responsive web or mobile app.
Live Lectures & Recordings
Integration with Zoom or MS Teams allows live classes to be scheduled within the LMS. Sessions are recorded and automatically uploaded for revision.
Performance Analytics
Dashboards show instructor feedback, quiz performance, and attendance data. Automated insights and predictive analytics help faculty identify at-risk students.

Content Management System (CMS)

News & Announcements
Admins publish open day announcements, departmental news, and academic calendars using a drag-and-drop page builder.
Departmental Pages
Each department manages its own microsite with tailored permissions, ensuring content is accurate and regularly updated.
Search & SEO Optimization
Built-in tools ensure all pages are optimized for search engines, including schema integration and dynamic sitemaps.
